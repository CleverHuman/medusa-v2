REFUND AND RESHIP POLICY

By using our services, you agree to these terms and conditions. Please read them carefully before making any purchases.

1. Acknowledgement

A client seeking refund / reship in relation to an order MUST read this section prior to contacting us. Upon raising a request of such kind the client is expected to have the following assumed knowledge:

  1. A tracking number that fails to retrieve information from tracking services, except for when it displays "invalid tracking number", does not mean the order is not sent.
  2. If the client raises a dispute in relation to an order, then the client forfeits the right to have the order investigated by us, and privileges mentioned in this section.
  3. Once a negative review is left by the client in relation to an order, then the order will not receive considerations mentioned in this section.
  4. A client has understood and agreed to matters mentioned herein prior to purchasing.

2. Refund / Reship Guidelines

This guideline serves only as a reference, and does not serve as a guarantee. We reserve the final decision in all reship/refund matters. By purchasing from our store you agree to respect those decisions.

An order may only be considered for refund/ reship when it satisfies condition(s) described in section 2.2 and 2.4.

Reship and/or refund decisions are made on a case-by-case basis, with the following criteria as considerations:

  • Client's purchase history
  • Client's credential
  • Client's perceived integrity
  • Client's future prospect with us
  • Value of the order

2.2 Non-Arrival

Except described in section 2.3, an order can be considered as a non-arrival if the tracking in which the order relates fails to cause a result from the server after 7 business days from the date in which the order was placed.

2.3 Non-Arrival Exceptions

The following tracking result does not constitute a "non-arrival":

  1. Delivery made to incorrect address (this will result in internal investigation to determine the causes, and decision made accordingly)
  2. Order is "returned to sender"
  3. If buyer chooses shipping options where tracking is not available and it fails to arrive

2.4 Non-Conformance

Except described in section 2.5, an order can be considered as non-conformant if:

  1. The order is less than the amount mentioned in the listing
  2. The client receives the incorrect product
  3. The product cannot reasonably be considered satisfactory

Please note: Given the amount of scammers we get trying to tell us that their order is short when trying to get freebies, we almost will not proceed with a non-conformance investigation unless the client is well known to us or the order is substantial.

2.5 Non-Conformance Exceptions

The following are not instances of non-conformance:

  1. The product is damaged during transit
  2. The product does not resemble that of the display photo

2.6 Refund by Cancellation

An order may be canceled under the following circumstances:

  1. The shipping address is outside of Australia
  2. The client is deemed unfit to make the purchase e.g. a person under the age of 18, a member of law enforcement, or is in risk of self harm or harming others. We are not liable to disclose our reasons for the cancellation
  3. Unforeseen circumstance
